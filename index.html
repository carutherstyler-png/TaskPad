Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

📋

Shared boards

Create boards and invite your team or household with a simple 6-character code. Everyone stays in sync in real time.

↻

Smart recurrence

Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly — or any interval you choose. Done tasks reschedule themselves automatically.

⏱

Duration tracking

Know how long tasks take so you can plan your day realistically. Track time in minutes, hours, or days.

🗂

Custom groups

Organise tasks into any groups that make sense for your life — Home, Work, Errands, Projects — you decide.

📅

Due date filters

Instantly see what's due today or overdue across all groups. No more scanning through everything to find urgent tasks.

📱

Works everywhere

Install as an app on your phone or use it in any browser. Real-time sync means changes appear instantly on all devices.
