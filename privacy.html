Docket

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

At Docket ("we", "us", or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app and website at dockethq.co.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Docket:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Task data — tasks, groups, notes, and due dates you create in the app
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device information — device type and operating system for compatibility

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Docket service
  • To sync your data across devices in real time
  • To enable shared boards with people you invite
  • To send important updates about the service

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which complies with industry-standard security practices. Task data is stored in Firebase Firestore and is only accessible to you and the members of your shared boards.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Firebase (Google) — for data storage and authentication
  • Legal requirements — if required by law

Shared Boards

When you create a shared board and invite other users, those users will be able to see all tasks and content within that board. Only share boards with people you trust.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your task data at any time using the CSV export feature

To request data deletion, email us at tyler@dockethq.co.

Children's Privacy

Docket is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at tyler@dockethq.co.
